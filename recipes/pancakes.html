Pancakes

Description

These are the best pancakes ever! They are light and fluffy and taste great with maple syrup. --> They are easy to make and are a great way to start your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

    • Recipe

    1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the egg, milk, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
    2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
