Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour

    • Recipe

    1. Gather all ingredients.
    2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C), and lightly grease a pizza pan
    3. In a large bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
    4. Stir in salt, olive oil, and 2 cups flour. Beat until smooth. Stir in enough remaining flour to make a soft dough.
    5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface, and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes.
    6. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, turning once to coat top. Cover with a damp cloth, and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size, about 1 hour.
    7. Punch dough down, and divide into 2 balls. Roll each ball out on a lightly floured surface to fit your pizza pan.
    8. Place dough on the prepared pizza pan. Top with your favorite pizza toppings, and bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown, about 15 minutes.
